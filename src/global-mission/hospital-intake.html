Patient Intake Guidelines

Structuring clinical registration, outpatient check-ins, and emergency admissions within an orderly and standardized institutional framework.

Onboarding Checklist & Protocols

To initiate a medical file registration or coordinate an inpatient admission at our Homedakrom facility, patients must fulfill these critical documentation standards:

1. Verified Identification

Valid national health insurance cards, national ID parameters, or verified congregational reference codes.

2. Historical Records

Previous diagnostic summaries, active medication lists, and specialized care tracking matrices.

3. Parental/Maternal Documentation

antenatal record cards, childhood immunization histories, and verified guardian metrics.

4. Welfare Matrix Alignment

Completed community aid documentation for subsidized healthcare delivery structures.
