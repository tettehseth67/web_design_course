Institutional Admissions Policy

We maintain an orderly framework for student entry, prioritizing academic curiosity, community accountability, and moral alignment.

1. Registration Criteria

Applications for entry into our Kindergarten and Basic Primary Schools are thoroughly evaluated by our administrative registry. New cohorts are integrated strictly at the start of calculated seasonal school terms.

2. Document Protocols

To initiate the student onboarding process, the Secretariat requires verified historical health records, official developmental tracking data from previous institutions, and completed parental covenant agreements.
